## What is weeklyOSM?

**OSM's Weekly News Source**
- For **everyone** - beginners to experts
- **15 languages** (translation volunteers essential)
- **Independent** of organizations/companies
- **52 issues per year** (no breaks!)

**https://weeklyosm.eu/**
